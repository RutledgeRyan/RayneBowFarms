Current Microgreens Available and Their Nutritional Description/Flavor Profile

Speckled Pea Microgreens

Nutritional Benefits: Speckled pea microgreens are a good source of vitamins A, C, and K. They also contain essential minerals like iron and potassium, as well as dietary fiber. These microgreens are known for their antioxidant properties and potential anti-inflammatory benefits.

Flavor Profile: Speckled pea microgreens have a mild, fresh flavor with hints of sweetness similar to fresh peas. They add a delicate crunch to dishes.

Broccoli Microgreens

Nutritional Benefits: Broccoli microgreens are incredibly nutritious and packed with vitamins A, C, and K. They also contain minerals like potassium, calcium, and iron. These microgreens are known for their potential cancer-fighting properties and high antioxidant content.

Flavor Profile: Broccoli microgreens have a milder and sweeter taste compared to mature broccoli. They offer a subtle earthy and fresh flavor. These microgreens are versatile and can be used in salads, smoothies, or as a garnish.

Rambo Radish Microgreens

Nutritional Benefits:Rambo radish microgreens are packed with essential nutrients. They are a great source of vitamins C, E, and K, and also provide minerals like calcium, potassium, and iron. These microgreens are known for their potential detoxifying properties and digestive benefits.

Flavor Profile: Rambo radish microgreens have a spicy and peppery flavor similar to full-grown radishes. They add a zesty kick and vibrant color to salads, sandwiches, and other dishes.

Kohlrabi Microgreens

Nutritional Benefits: Kohlrabi microgreens are highly nutritious and rich in vitamins C, E, and K. They also provide minerals like potassium and calcium. These microgreens are known for their potential immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory properties.

Flavor Profile: Kohlrabi microgreens have a mild, slightly sweet flavor with a hint of cabbage-like taste. They add a crisp texture and subtle flavor to salads, wraps, and stir-fries.

Black Oil Sunflower Microgreens

Nutritional Benefits: Black oil sunflower microgreens are a nutrient-dense choice. They are a good source of vitamins A, B complex, C, and E. These microgreens contain minerals like iron, calcium, and selenium. They are known for their potential cardiovascular health benefits and antioxidant properties.

Flavor Profile: Black oil sunflower microgreens have a nutty and mild flavor with a hint of fresh sunflower seeds. They add a pleasant crunch and nuttiness to salads, sandwiches, and soups.
